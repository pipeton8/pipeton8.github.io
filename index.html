I am a Mathematician and Economist from PUC-Chile. My current interests lie in the applications of AI and Machine Learning to social sciences problems, especially in Economics.

In particular, I am looking to study: 1) the use of state-of-the-art Machine and Deep learning algorithms to make sense of unstructured data like text, images, videos, or sounds; 2) learn how to make causal inference with these tools, and 3) understand the role of these techniques in our society, specifically how they preserve our biases.
